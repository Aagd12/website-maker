About Us

To accelerate the efforts to achieve universal sanitation coverage and to put focus on sanitation, the Prime Minister of India had launched the Swachh Bharat Mission on 2nd October 2014.

We are a group of students of Indian Institute of Information Technology, Guwahati, who have came up with an idea to campaign for the Swachchha Bharat Abhiyaan on institute level. We used technology to create a discussion forum for our institute to have some fruitful conversation on cleanliness and sanitation. We have also added some blogs on cleanliness which we keep updating from time to time so that our readers can learn from them as well as to keep them interested in the campaign. We have also added learn section in our site so that interested users can learn something useful on various cleanliness practices and techniques.
